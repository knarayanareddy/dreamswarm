# Code of Conduct

## Our Pledge
We as members, contributors, and maintainers pledge to make participation in DreamSwarm a harassment-free experience for everyone, regardless of age, body size, disability, ethnicity, sex characteristics, gender identity, level of experience, education, socio-economic status, nationality, personal appearance, race, religion, or sexual identity and orientation.

## Our Standards
**Positive behaviors**:
- Using welcoming and inclusive language.
- Being respectful of differing viewpoints and experiences.
- Gracefully accepting constructive criticism.
- Focusing on what is best for the community.
- Showing empathy towards other community members.

**Unacceptable behaviors**:
- Trolling, insulting comments, and personal attacks.
- Harassment of any kind, public or private.
- Publishing others' private information without consent.
- Sustained disruption of discussion.
- Other conduct reasonably considered inappropriate in a professional setting.

## Enforcement
Community leaders will follow these steps:
1. **Correction**: Private written warning with clarity on what was inappropriate.
2. **Warning**: Public warning with consequences for continued behavior.
3. **Temporary Ban**: Temporary removal from community interaction.
4. **Permanent Ban**: Permanent removal from community spaces.

## Reporting
Report unacceptable behavior to: `conduct@dreamswarm.dev`
All reports will be reviewed and investigated promptly and fairly. Community leaders are obligated to respect the privacy and security of the reporter.
