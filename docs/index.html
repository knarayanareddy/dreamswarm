KAIROS Daemon

A heartbeat that never stops.

KAIROS (Kinetic AI Real-time Operational System) is a persistent Tokio-powered daemon that monitors your workspace 24/7. When it detects a missing test, an anti-pattern, or a predicted merge conflict, it acts — with trust-gated autonomy.



As KAIROS proves its competence, it graduates from AskFirst → AutoAct — handling low-risk improvements without interrupting your flow.
